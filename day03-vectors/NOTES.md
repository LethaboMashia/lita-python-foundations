# Day 3 — Vectors & Cosine Similarity


## What is a vector?
A vector is an ordered list where positioning actually matters

## What does the dot product measure?
The dot product multiplies matching positions and add one number showing how much two vectors overlap, but bigger vectors score higher just for being bigger.

## Why divide by the magnitudes?
We divide by the magnitudes because when calculating the dot product we only want the direction so this helps us filter it
